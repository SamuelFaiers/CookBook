Utensils

There are a wide variety of utensils you will need for your kitchen. As with cookware, it is best to slowly build up your supplies as you need them. That is, once you have the following essentials.

Plastic Spatula

To lift and flip food without damaging non-stick cookware.

Wooden Spoon

For stirring food, nothing more, nothing less.

Slotted Spoon

To extract food from cooking liquid without draining it.

Tongs

For lifting pieces of food that are too large for a spatula or slotted spoon.

Whisk

To blend indgredients together or introduce air into a mixture.

Microplane Grater

For grating food, especially cheese. A box grater is an alternative option.

Peeler

To quickly and easily peel fruits and vegetables.

Can Opener

Its purpose is in the name.

Colander

This is essential for pasta dishes where you will need to drain away the water.

Sieve

To quickly seperate solids from liquids and to sift ingredients like flour.

Measuring Spoons

For when a recipe calls for measurement like table or tea spoons.

Measuring Jug

To measure specific volumes of liquid when a recipe calls for such.
