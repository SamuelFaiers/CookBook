Frying

Of all cooking methods, frying is what you will likely use the most. While being a form of dry-heat cooking, it is so versatile that it gets its own page.

See Frying

Dry-heat cooking

Cooking methods that do not utilise water to heat food are classified as dry-heat cooking methods. They result in the Maillard reaction, more commonly known as browning.

See Dry-heat

Moist-heat Cooking

Methods of cooking that do utilise water or any other liquid are classified as moist-heat cooking methods. This is healthier than dry-heat cooking methods as it does not require any additional oil or fat.

See Moist-heat
